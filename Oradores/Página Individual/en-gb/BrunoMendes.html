Bruno Mendes


Project Manager A400M Training Devices and Simulators

The World of Simulation

Wednesday, 13:00




Biografy


Bruno Mendes is currently managing the Airbus A400M Training Aids and Simulators delivery to Royal Air Force in Airbus Defence and Space.
Bruno Mendes started his carrier in Empordef-TI as an Aerospace / Systems Engineer developing simulation models and continued to do so in Germany, first in Altran and then for Airbus until 2012. After that, he returned to Altran in Spain and stood as a Full Flight Simulator Project Manager before joining his current job.
Several projects passed through him, Tactical Manoeuver Tank Simulator for Portuguese Army - Pandur II, Tiger Helicopter Maintenance Trainer for German and French Air Force and also A400M Full Flight Simulator.
