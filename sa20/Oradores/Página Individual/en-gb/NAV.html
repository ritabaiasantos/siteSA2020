Biography


Currently, Portugal is the main country from Europe that is showing the greatest growth in the tourism and travel sector. This puts an enormous pressure on airport systems that inevitably have to provide a safe and responsible response that aims to improve efficiency. Join us and position yourself at the forefront of the restructuring of European airspace, with all the experience and knowledge of Carlos Terenas, present Head of the Terminal and Approximation of Lisbon, Jesús Conde, current position in the Department of Airspace at NAV Portugal, and Nuno Nepomuceno, Security liaison officer.
