Tiago Baptista


Head of Artificial Intelligence




Biography


Tiago Baptista graduated in Informatics in the University of Coimbra. Nowadays he is responsible for Critical Software’s Department of Artificial Intelligence, having more than 20 years of experience in Software Engineering and research in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, high performance computation, artificial life and complex systems.





Christian Perone


Senior Machine Learning Researcher




Biography


Christian Perone graduated in the Polytechnic of Montreal with a Master’s Degree in Biomedical Engineering and Deep Learning, one type of Machine Learning. Nowadays he’s an investigator and a Machine Learning Engineer at Jungle. During his talk Christian Perone will expose the technical limitations of Artificial Intelligence, the main focus of the research undertaken in this area.





Rodrigo Ventura


Assistant Professor




Biography


Rodrigo Ventura, having graduated in IST with a Bachelor’s Degree in Electrotechnical Engineering, achieved in the same institution a Master’s Degree and a Doctorate in the area of artificial intelligence. Nowadays he’s an assistant professor in the department of electrotechnical and computer engineering. Rodrigo Ventura will share with us an historical and social perspective of Artificial Intelligence, as well as recent developments in the area of Space Robotics, where the areas of AI and Robotics meet.
