What data do we collect?

For the event, we collect personal data and process it when:
- You upload your CV on our curriculum bank;
- You sign up for our event.

When you visit our website, we do not collect personal data. We only have access to statistics, and therefore to pseudonymized data.

The entity responsible for processing the data is Associação Portuguesa de Aeronáutica e Espaço, based in Avenida Rovisco Pais, nº1, 1049-001 Lisboa, registered in Conservatória do Registo Comercial de Lisboa, with the single registration number and of Collective Person 503 908 380, as a non-profit association

Query here all data that is collected during the event, as well as with which companies such data is being shared in the case of the curriculum bank.




What are my rights?

Under the legislation in force, in particular the Portuguese Law No 58/2019 of 8 August and the EU regulation 2016/679, the holder of personal data has:
- Right to access the data
- Right to rectification of data
- Right to erase data
- Right to limitation of treatment
- Right of opposition

Query the page on the rights of personal data subjects of the (Portuguese) National Data Protection Commission for more information (page in portuguese).

The Semana Aeroespacial team informs you that you can exercise your rights by communicating it to the email address semana.aeroespacial@aerotec.tecnico.ulisboa.pt
