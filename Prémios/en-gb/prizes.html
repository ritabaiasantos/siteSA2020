Prémios




Experiences

Tired of jumping to hasty conclusions about the result in the exam? Take advantage of the more than 50 interconnected trampolines available at our partner's premises. You can win this 50% discount voucher by visiting the Exhibit.




Bored of the monotony of the semester? Enter a new reality, lock yourself in a room and solve all the puzzles, be quick and try to escape. This is what you can win by going to our lectures and visiting the Exhibit, using one of the gift vouchers that allow you to have access to a free experience for up to 5 people.




After a good barbecue at the IST, are you still going to spend more money on transportation? Hitchhike with Bolt to get home well, and in the cheapest way using our Discount Code SA2020. You can win our €7 discount vouchers by visiting the Exhibit.




Visiting the Exhibit you can win a month of free Yoga classes! Learn breathing techniques and relaxation exercises to master the next semesters. Stay tuned to our social networks to participate in our Yoga workshop, to be held in the future.


The Aerospace Week team takes you to fly along with the Vertical Club to the clouds, literally! You can win a twin-paragliding flight, by going to the Exhibit and the lectures, so you can briefly forget about the burden of academic work and to have an unforgettable experience.




It is proven that physical exercise helps us to conserve our sanity, something that is very important to maintain during a complicated semester. So when you feel saturated with your studies, you can enjoy a week of free gym on LGC facilities, a prize you win visiting our Exhibit.
